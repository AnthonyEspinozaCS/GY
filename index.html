Roofing & Building Solutions

Mission Statement

To provide an exit strategy for business owners that are looking to retire and maintain a positive and growing relationship with existing employees, customers, vendors, lenders and their communities by continuing their legacy.

Who are we

George Yancci combines cutting-edge roofing technology with traditional craftsmanship to deliver high-quality, sustainable, and durable roofing solutions. We're not just roofers - we're partners in protecting your home. With our unparalleled customer service, comprehensive warranties, and commitment to the community, we provide not just a roof but a roofing experience that brings peace of mind, knowing you've chosen the best for your home or business.

Our Vision

To firmly establish ourselves as the unrivaled leader in succession strategies for the roofing and construction industry across the entirety of the United States. Our aim is to be recognized as the gold standard, ensuring the seamless transition of businesses and the preservation of legacies while contributing to the ongoing growth and innovation of the industry.

Our Culture

  • Customer Satisfaction Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to exceed their expectations and provide services that bring them peace of mind and value for their investment.

  • Empowerment & Collaboration We value our employees, customers, and partners, promoting growth, teamwork, and mutual respect. We believe in strong partnerships and collaborative relationships with all stakeholders.

  • Quality Craftsmanship We are committed to delivering high-quality workmanship in every project. We use the best materials and methods, ensuring the longevity and durability of our roofing solutions.

  • Community & Sustainability We acknowledge our role as community members and strive to create a positive impact through sustainable business practices. We are committed to the prosperity and growth of our communities and our responsibility towards future generations.

Geographic Overview

At George Yancci Roofing and Building Solutions, we are commencing our journey in the vibrant construction market of Texas. As we navigate across the Southern U.S., we aim to harness the region's dynamic industry potential, ultimately anchoring ourselves firmly within the unique building landscape of Florida. Our grand vision is to extend our reach across the United States, weaving together local businesses into the larger tapestry of the George Yancci family. Our strategic roadmap will elevate George Yancci into a dominant player in the national roofing and building solutions sector, utilizing local proficiency to offer outstanding services on a national scale.
